Lanparty Review - LANSynergy I

07 Feb 2003 | lanparty LANSynergy I

LANSynergy I

Introduction

TheCyberWolf - They’ve done it again… An excelent lan party done at a large scale. And as always this one was the BEST yet. How can that be? you ask since the last one was “the best”, well let me see if we can explain.

Stability

Rating 9.0

Sleggs (GoDS Guest) - The lan party was awsome. most of the people arrived around 7:00. everyone had a great time showing off their most recent mods, crazy case designs, and witty t-shirt slogans. all the tournements went off without a hitch. everyone had a great time fragging their friends and winning prizes. with a full stock of bawls and plenty of pizza to go around nothing could stop us from gaming to the max. i had a great time and am definitly planning of attending in the future.

ReUseability

Rating 10

CyberFireX - I thought that this was an excellent party and one that shouldn’t have been missed. It was an excellent outting for the first go as Lan Synergy, and aside from one theft really didn’t have much in the way of bad events. I was a bit unhappy with the total non availablility of drinks except for beer. They should have ditched the snow cones and actually gotten drinks. They seemed to have the power situation almost completely undercontrol (except for poor cyberwolfs table) :-), and the network was actually fairly stable though still not fast. Excelent prizes, interesting concept of having the tourneys run by the attending clans. I had a good time and can’t wait for LanS 2!

Compatability

Rating 8.0

Abysal - There were plenty of games up at all times, but with about 100 people that should be expected. As far as the people go, most were pretty cool, however there was one incident were someone’s laptop was taken. That goes to show you that even though we are all gamerz, this still was a public event and you should watch your stuff. Someone from the [GoDS] group was always around keeping an eye out when ever the others were out at WAWA or where ever.

Grafix

Rating 9.0

Abysal - The actual tournaments of the event were fairly well scheduled and setup. One problem was that dedicated servers seemed to be a royalty. Luckily I brought my server with me and after the initial time to load and Config the server, I had UT2003 ready to go for the tourneys, a Big thanks to XP for their help. The other problem that affected more people was the network latency. Allot of times you would see pings in the 75 - 90 ms range. I think most of us expect < 30 ms pings in games. Although honestly you really didn’t notice it too much. Once again DoomXexec311 took the CS MVP title, although he had some good competition from “gsg Bruce”. Other then that [GoDS] did not take any other titles.

FX

10.0

Abysal - Power, power, and plenty of power! I must congratulate the LAN Synergy team for doing a superb job on power distribution. There was only one outage due to someone plugging into the wall rather then plugging into the allocated power cables. However I do not know the full severity since I slept through it :p

Network

Rate: 8.5

Abysal - The network was fairly quick taking into account the amount of people we had with all of the games + other misc traffic going on. File transfer speed on my switch segment was great, I know my self and other [GoDS] members were on a 100 Megabit switch/hub. However I think those were hubs, and that is why network lag was so high in games as mentioned above.

Overall

Rate: 9

Abysal - LAN Synergy usually provides or allows gamers to bring alcohol. In the past I did not really care for this idea. But as with the last 3 previous parties there were no incidents at this one. Even 8-Ball was un characteristically quite. So I think I have accepted the idea of alcohol at these parties, I even found my self drinking a good amount… As far as food goes, pizza was provided, about 2 - 3 slices per person. Non-Alcohol drinks were not provided, and I think most groups brought drinks for themselves. Also, cost wise the party was a bit expensive @ $35 per person. But since the last parties ran over the budget, this was to be expected. Over all a very well planned party.

Conclusion

TheCyberWolf - Well I hope this helps explain wht this was “the best” lanparty but if it didn’t then all I can say is you have to be there for the Next one and figure it out for yourself. See you all at the next one and remeber PRE-PAY GUARENTEES YOU A SEAT!!!

Lanparty Review - LAN2K1 v4

28 Mar 2002 | lanparty LAN2K1

LAN2K1

Introduction

BIGGER. BETTER. DA BOMB!!! This is the second time me and Abysal went to Lan2k1 and it just gets better each time. Well this time we brought backup. 12 of us were there and we showed them what it means to be a GOD!!! Since so many GODS showed up I decided to get some input from them too.

Stability

Rating 9.0

3 parties, 60+ people ( I dunno if we ever got a FINAL count of how many), and WHOLE HECK OF ALOT OF BAWLS!!! We can be as loud as we want… never have to worry about being crowded… and frag and frag to our little hearts content. This place is great but I really think we are begining to see the max possiblity of this location. Not to say 60+ isn’t a good number but I dunno where they are gonna put people, let alone get power for them.

ReUseability

Rating 8.9

Yo, I had a great time at this LAN. Was expecting more competition plus more tournaments to occur but they all didn’t due to time. Anyhow it was great owning it up :) I’m glad I won the p4 chip and mobo…. muwhaha. Till next LAN hopefully more tournaments will happen. Can’t wait >:) -DoomXexec311-

I would definitely come back to lan 2k1. There was a lot going on and always 2 or 3 different games to play. There were lots of cool people. A couple of annoying people, but that’s to be expected. I definitely would recommend it to everyone. Just a tip though, treat your stuff as if you were leaving it out on the sidewalk, this is a public event not a friendly one. Keep an eye on everything, and don’t leave your stuff lying about. -CyberFireX-

Compatability

Rating 8.0

Well it was bound to happen. The more people you add to the mix the more of a chance that 1 of them will piss someone off. Luckly it was handled very quietly and I believe all in volved realized what they did wasnot the best thing to do. For those not in the know… Don’t worry about it. It was nothing, just keep in mind that this is a big even and people from all over attend these. It’s not a party of friends and what you think is funny might piss someone else off.

Grafix

Rating 9.0

The projection screen made a come back and was nice to watch. No Fog or Disco lights which was good.

FX

8.0

Everything was fine, UNTIL… (Blinding light!!!) Yep some one managed to find the light switch and take down half the GODS crew. I know we were kicking some butt, but come one… there has got to be a better way to beat us without blowing a circuit breaker. Just kidding!!! -CyberFireX-

The prizes were awesome, and the raffle was a good idea. The fact that he gave away the best prizes as raffles meant that the games were more for fun than bitter competition. The observer view screen was a great idea, it let people watch the battle without crowding the gamers. The network was great. My only real complaint was that the microphone they were using was highly inadequate, they should consider putting speakers around the room for that, or multiple boxes, and doing rogerwilco. They had a great idea with posting an intranet webpage, and putting common drivers, and game patches up there so that everyone could get to them.

Network

Rate: 9.5

As usual an impressive setup. Fiber uplinks were nice but we noticed that the 3 Switches were not in use, only 2. Dunno what happened… not sure anyone really does but it’s just an observasion. In LAN2k1’s defense they ran good old CAT5 to the GODS section and all was good. The “overflow” area(I think it was up front) made good use of my 16 port switch. I was happy to help. OH speaking of HELP. I met some great guys at Lan2k1… Why? cause they were haveing some problems getting connected. While I don’t mind helping (and these guys were great guys to hang with too) I do get just a little upset when people don’t test their hardware. The problem boiled down to a bad Cat5 cable they brought from home. Like I said I didn’t mind helping but please… PLEASE!!!…. check your stuff BEFORE you come to a party. It saves time and you’ll get into the game faster.

Overall

Rate: 10

I love lan parties, I can’t imagine how boring my life would be with out them. I’ve been to Lan parties before, but I gotta say, lan2k1 throws the best ones! Especially this time around, there was food, plenty of drinks, and tons, tons of gaming! Can it really get any better then this? My friends and I also host lan parties on a monthly basis, and I hope that one day we can put something togther like lan2k1 did. Great job lan2k1! -=AbysaL=-

Conclusion

I’m glad 8-Ball made an apperance, dunno if it would have been the same with out him. Well Lan2k1 has done it again. I hope they keep doing it too for years to come. Games, Tourneys, Meatballs (which were great)… we got it all and Bawls to wash it all down. Can’t wait till v5.0 AND the big party at Greenfields Fire Hall (Watch for it on LAN2k1’s site). I’ll see ya at the next lanparty!

Lanparty Review - Cramped Combat E1

28 Mar 2002 | lanparty Cramped Combat E1

Cramped Combat E1

Introduction

Cramped Lan. Sounds uncomfortable but it was suprizingly spacious. The name came from the original location in which REAL cramped. It could have gotten cramped but we had quite enough room even though.

Stability

Rating 9.0

Everything was good. No fights, arguments or anything… well except for which game to play next.

ReUseability

Rating 8.9

This was very fun. It was just a bunch of guys playin and having fun. The food was definatly a nice touch but I’m sure it got expensive for the host. I would definately come back even if they don’t offer the food.

Compatability

Rating 8.0

Definately an open atmosphere. I liked the fact that we were not banished to a basement and reminded to “keep it down”. We were in the heart of the house (dining area, spilling into the kitchen) and not once did anyone tell me to keep it down. Now normaly I would not be offended even IF they did tell me to lower my voice, because I do know I am naturally very loud.

Grafix

Rating 9.0

n/a

FX

8.0

I didn’t find this out till later in the lanparty but a laptop with a simple web cam was recording pix every minute. It’s a simple idea but I like it … It’s not novel, Lan2k1 did it before… It’s not very Hi-Tech… but you know what … It’s a cool idea and I like it … always have and always will.

Network

Rate: 9.5

Network was fine. Internet Access was a nice touch and was bearly abused, which is always good. I would recommend a few dedicated servers but otherwise it was fine.

Overall

Rate: 10

The Lan Was great. The people and atmosphere were good too. When you have all this it’s hard to not have a good time.

Conclusion

I can’t wait till there is another lan like this. It was just more fun.
